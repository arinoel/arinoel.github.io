The Dog Book

The Dog Book is a project that I worked on in my final semester of graduate school. The book comes in both print and digital formats, created from a manuscript of prose poetry in an epistolary format. The project focused on creating an efficient print-to-digital workflow; both the print PDF and the ePub were exported from the same InDesign file.
